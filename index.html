The Horcruxes will be given a random strength at the start of the game.

There are four horcruxes below. By clicking on a horcrux you will inflict a specific amount of damage.

You win the game by exactly matching your 'Damage Points' to the 'Horcrux Strength', you lose the game if do more damage than necessary.

The Damage Points of each horcrux are hidden from you.

Each time when the game starts, the Horcruxes' Damage Points will change.
